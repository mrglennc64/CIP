05 — How CIP Works

A deterministic, repeatable five-stage pipeline.

01

Surface Enumeration

Identifies all reachable comms surfaces — web, email, IVR, metadata, and API endpoints.

02

Channel Diagnostics

Runs deterministic checks across 9 channels with no heuristic or marketing-based scoring.

03

Risk Classification

Assigns CX Impact and Fix Priority (P1 / P2 / P3) to every finding.

04

Procurement Readiness Score

Aggregates the CCaaS, UCaaS, AI, and Compliance pillars into a single readiness score.

05

Drift Detection

Weekly re-scans detect metadata drift, DNS changes, CTA changes, and IVR modifications.
